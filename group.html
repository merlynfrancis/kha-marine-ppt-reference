Why the Group Exists

Why the group exists.

The group is structured so that major projects can be delivered turnkey. Vessels from KHA Marine, interiors from Montaigne Design, facility operations from SERVA, smart systems from DNAKE, cabinetry from Suofeiya, all underwritten by MANA Advisory and built with Build-Tech Pro systems.

One commercial relationship. One accountable group. A single point of contact for the kind of integrated mandates that would otherwise need a half-dozen vendors and a project office to coordinate them.
