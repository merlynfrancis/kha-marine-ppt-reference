Why Fiberglass

The right material for Gulf waters.

Built for the Gulf's demanding marine environment, fiberglass offers a structural advantage over traditional materials. Unlike metal hulls, it does not suffer from electrochemical corrosion, reducing long-term maintenance costs and eliminating the need for constant protective treatments.

Its layered composite structure allows engineers to optimize strength-to-weight ratio, improving fuel efficiency while maintaining durability under high-stress conditions. This precision reinforcement ensures better impact resistance and structural integrity in critical load zones.

For patrol fleets that run continuously, commercial operators that can't afford downtime, and private owners who expect long service life — composite construction outperforms.
