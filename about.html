About KHA Marine

Marine engineering, built for demanding waters.

KHA Marine is a UAE-rooted marine engineering and manufacturing company building high-performance fiberglass vessels for government, commercial, industrial, and private clients across the Gulf.

Our Story

About KHA Marine.

KHA Marine is a marine engineering and manufacturing company delivering high-performance fiberglass vessels built for demanding environments. We specialize in custom boat building, structural fiberglass solutions, and end-to-end marine support, combining precision engineering with practical, field-tested durability.

From concept to completion, every build is driven by performance, reliability, and long-term value. Our team works closely with clients to design and manufacture vessels that meet exact operational requirements — whether for commercial, industrial, or specialized marine use. With a focus on quality materials, efficient production, and Gulf-ready construction standards, KHA Marine delivers solutions that perform where it matters most: on the water.

Mission & Vision

Why KHA Marine exists.

A clear mandate, executed in fiberglass. From the first consultation to the final sea trial.

Mission

What we do.

To design, manufacture, and maintain fiberglass boats built for the Gulf's demanding waters — serving government, commercial, and private clients with precision and trust.

Vision

Where we're going.

To become one of the Gulf region's most trusted marine manufacturing brands — recognised for durable fiberglass boats, purposeful design, and long-term operational reliability.
