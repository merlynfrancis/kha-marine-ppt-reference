Fiberglass Manufacturing

Marine-grade composite construction. Built to inspect.

Every KHA Marine vessel begins as engineered fiberglass — laid by hand, reinforced where load concentrates, and finished to a standard that survives Gulf temperature, salinity, and operational stress.

Our manufacturing program supports patrol fleets, commercial operators, and private owners who need vessels that hold their finish, retain structural integrity, and remain in service.
